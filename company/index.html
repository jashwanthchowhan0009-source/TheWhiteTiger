Why we exist

Vision & Mission

The modern professional hunts through a dozen biased sources just to find one reliable fact. We were founded to end that — by building the information infrastructure India deserves.

Our Vision

To build India's premier data infrastructure — processing global, real-time information streams to strip out bias and noise, and delivering absolute Information Sovereignty and unfiltered, objective truth directly to every user's personalised feed.

Our Mission

To architect and scale SherrByte into India's most trusted, professional-grade information platform — leveraging high-fidelity data engineering to replace clickbait and editorial bias with verifiable, real-time intelligence tailored for every professional.

What TheWhiteTiger is

“The White Tiger is a data-intelligence powerhouse that processes global internet data and media streams into high-fidelity, actionable insights — eliminating noise and editorial bias to ensure verifiable, real-time data delivery and absolute Information Sovereignty for every user.”

We are an information & data services company. Artificial intelligence is a tool we use inside the platform — for deduplication, summarisation, semantic embedding, classification and verification — not the business itself. The business is clean, structured, trustworthy information, delivered to people and to enterprises.

What's inside

Engineered for information integrity

Every architectural decision is optimised for factual accuracy and bias elimination — not engagement maximisation.

Abstractive Synthesis

AI-generated WWWW (Who · What · Where · Why) summaries that capture the facts of a story without reproducing sensationalist framing.

Hallucination Control

RAG-consensus cross-verification checks every AI summary against its source material to prevent factual drift.

Source Diversity

The MMR re-ranker ensures no single publisher dominates your feed — protecting against filter bubbles by design.

Real-Time Personalisation

EMA profile vectors + Redis Streams update your interests within minutes of every click, read, save or share.

Semantic Discovery

A pgvector HNSW index enables sub-millisecond similarity search — finding thematically related stories across sources.

SGI Watermarking

Every AI-generated summary is transparently marked, in full compliance with India's IT Rules 2026.

The build

Engineered across six structured phases

From a FastAPI foundation to a production-ready, compliant platform — and a clear roadmap beyond.

Phase A–B

Foundation & Security Complete

FastAPI scaffolding, Dockerfile, CI/CD, health endpoints, Argon2id hashing, JWT rotation, rate limiting and secure sessions.

Phase C

Data Ingestion Pipeline Complete

Real-time RSS ingestion from 24+ verified global & Indian publishers, custom deduplication, full-text extraction and copyright-compliant parsing.

Phase D

AI Intelligence Layer Complete

Gemini→Groq summarisation cascade, MiniLM embeddings, pgvector HNSW index, WWWW abstractive summarisation and the 9-pillar taxonomy.

Phase E

Personalisation Engine Complete

User interaction tables, Redis Streams event buffer, EMA profile vectors, hybrid content + collaborative scorer and an MMR re-ranker.

Phase F

PWA Frontend Complete

A dark, editorial, glassmorphic Progressive Web App — mobile-responsive and production-deployable.

Phase G

Production Deployment In progress

Mumbai-region targeting for low latency, Render hot-standby failover and full platform monitoring.

Phase 2 · Post-funding

Bytes · Circles · B2B API · Multilingual Next

Short-form Bytes, VIBGYOR community Circles, the productised B2B Core Data API, and Telugu / Hindi multilingual support.
